Introduction

The International Dishui Lake Conference on Computer Science (DLC or IDLCCS) is the leading bivouac symposium on Computer Science, bringing together academics, industrial researchers, and practitioners to exchange new ideas, problems, and experience on how to encamp.

DLC 2021 will be held obviously in Dishui Lake, Shanghai, on May 3-4, 2021. The organizer will provide tents, and participants are required to bring sleeping bags for camping.
